Together referred to as the "**Parties**".

---

### 1. Background

1.1. Owner operates the **MyMultiverse** software studio and is the sole owner of
the studio and all of its intellectual property.

1.2. Contributor provided product vision, priorities, user-experience direction,
feedback on flows and copy, and related advisory input for the **Ammò** application
(household nutrition logistics) (the "**Contributions**").

1.3. Owner developed and owns the Ammò software, brand assets, domains, and all
related materials. The Parties enter into this Agreement to confirm that all
intellectual property arising from the Contributions vests in and remains with Owner.

---

### 2. Assignment of intellectual property

2.1. Contributor hereby **assigns, transfers, and conveys** to Owner all right, title,
and interest — present and future — in and to the Contributions, including any
copyright, design rights, database rights, and all other intellectual property rights
arising from work performed in connection with Ammò and MyMultiverse.

2.2. To the extent any right cannot be assigned by operation of law, Contributor
**irrevocably waives** such rights to the fullest extent permitted and agrees not to
assert them against Owner or any successor or licensee.

2.3. Contributor shall, on Owner's reasonable request and at Owner's cost, execute
further documents required to perfect or record Owner's title (for example, for
trademark or copyright registry filings).

---

### 3. Consideration

3.1. In consideration of the assignment in Section 2, Owner shall provide Contributor
with **public credit** for her role as product design and direction contributor on the
Ammò application, in the form described in Section 4 below.

3.2. Contributor confirms that no monetary fees, royalties, equity, or further
compensation are due under this Agreement. The public credit constitutes full and
final consideration for the assignment.

3.3. Contributor acknowledges that she has **no ownership interest** in MyMultiverse,
Ammò, the Ammò logo, the MyMultiverse name, source code, databases, domains, or
revenue from the product.

3.4. Contributor is **not** a co-founder, shareholder, or employee of MyMultiverse
unless a separate written instrument expressly states otherwise.

---

### 4. Public attribution

4.1. Owner shall credit Contributor in relevant public-facing materials (such as the
Ammò product page and app store listing) in a form such as:

> *"Product design and direction: Carola Zeno"*

4.2. Owner retains sole discretion over the precise wording, placement, and timing of
the credit, provided it clearly identifies Contributor's role in product design and
direction.

4.3. Public credit does **not** grant Contributor any ownership, licence, or
endorsement rights in the marks **MyMultiverse™** or **Ammò™**, nor any right to
represent herself as an owner, co-founder, or officer of MyMultiverse.

---

### 5. Confidentiality

5.1. Contributor shall keep confidential any non-public technical, business, strategic,
or user information learned during her involvement with Ammò, and shall not disclose
such information to third parties without Owner's prior written consent, except where
required by applicable law.

5.2. This obligation survives termination of the Parties' collaboration indefinitely.

---

### 6. Representations and warranties

6.1. Contributor represents and warrants that:

- (a) the Contributions are original to her or she had the right to make and assign them;
- (b) she has not granted, and will not grant, any conflicting rights over the Contributions to third parties; and
- (c) the Contributions do not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights.

6.2. Owner represents and warrants that he is the sole owner of MyMultiverse and has
full authority to enter into and perform this Agreement.

---

### 7. Governing law and jurisdiction

This Agreement is governed by the laws of **Spain**, without regard to conflict-of-law
rules. The courts of **Barcelona** shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any dispute
arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, subject to any mandatory consumer
or employment protections that may apply in Contributor's country of residence.

---

### 8. Entire agreement

8.1. This document constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties on the subject
of intellectual property arising from Contributor's involvement with Ammò and
supersedes all prior informal or oral understandings on ownership.

8.2. Any amendment to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by both Parties.

---
