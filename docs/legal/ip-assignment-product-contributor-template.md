**Agreement date:** [DATE]

2. **Carola ZENO** ("**Contributor**"), with address [ADDRESS], email [EMAIL].

---

### 1. Background

1.1. Owner operates the **MyMultiverse** software studio and is the sole owner of
the studio and its intellectual property.

1.2. Contributor acted as **product owner** for the **Ammò** application (household
nutrition logistics), providing product vision, priorities, user-experience
direction, feedback on flows and copy, and related advisory input (the
"**Contributions**").

1.3. Owner developed and owns the Ammò software, brand assets, domains, and
related materials. The parties wish to confirm that all intellectual property
remains with Owner.

---

### 2. Assignment of intellectual property

2.1. Contributor hereby **assigns, transfers, and conveys** to Owner all right,
title, and interest in and to the Contributions, including any copyright,
design rights, and other intellectual property rights arising from work
performed in connection with Ammò and MyMultiverse.

2.2. To the extent any right cannot be assigned by operation of law, Contributor
**irrevocably waives** such rights and agrees not to assert them against Owner.

2.3. Contributor shall, on request, execute further documents reasonably required
to perfect Owner's title (e.g. for trademark or copyright records).

---

### 3. No ownership or compensation (unless separately agreed)

3.1. Contributor acknowledges that she has **no ownership interest** in MyMultiverse,
Ammò, the Ammò logo, the MyMultiverse name, source code, databases, domains,
or revenue from the product, **unless** a separate written agreement expressly
states otherwise.

3.2. [OPTION A — no separate payment:] Contributor confirms that any past
involvement was [voluntary / compensated as follows: _________] and that no
further fees are due under this Agreement.

[OPTION B — one-time fee:] Owner shall pay Contributor €[AMOUNT] as full and
final consideration for the assignment in Section 2. Upon payment, no further
royalties or equity are owed.

3.3. Contributor is **not** a co-founder, shareholder, or employee of MyMultiverse
unless a separate instrument says so.

---

### 4. Public attribution (optional)

4.1. Owner may, at his discretion, credit Contributor in marketing materials, for
example: *"Product direction: Carola Zeno"* or *"Created with product direction
by Carola Zeno."*

4.2. Public credit does **not** grant Contributor any ownership, license, or
endorsement rights in the marks **MyMultiverse** or **Ammò**.

---

### 5. Confidentiality

5.1. Contributor shall keep confidential any non-public technical, business, or
user information learned during involvement with Ammò, except as required by law
or with Owner's written consent.

---

### 6. Representations

6.1. Contributor represents that her Contributions are original to her or she
had the right to grant this assignment, and that she has not granted conflicting
rights to third parties.

6.2. Owner represents that he is the sole owner of MyMultiverse and entitled to
receive this assignment.

---

### 7. Governing law and jurisdiction

This Agreement is governed by the laws of **[Italy / other]**. Courts of

This document is the entire agreement on IP relating to Contributor's involvement
